River Rafting Company

Author: Greg Nielson

Overview

Purpose

The purpose is to create an online place for people to gather with a shared interest in rafting. It would be a place for people to share their experiences and ask questions - let experience rafters share their expertise and ideas and let beginners ask questions about how to get started, where to go, and how to enjoy rafting. It would also be a place for people to trade or market equipment that may be otherwise hard to find or expensive.

Audience

The target audience would be both experienced rafters and beginners wishing to try it or get into it. Site should be inviting and usable both on computer and mobile devices to encourage frequent, flexible, convenient participation.
